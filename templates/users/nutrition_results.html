Sample Daily Meal Structure

Breakfast

~25% of daily calories

  • Oatmeal with berries
  • Eggs or protein source
  • Whole grain toast

Mid-Morning Snack

~10% of daily calories

  • Greek yogurt
  • Fruit or nuts
  • Protein shake

Lunch

~30% of daily calories

  • Lean protein (chicken, fish, tofu)
  • Complex carbs (rice, sweet potato)
  • Vegetables and salad

Pre-Workout Snack

~10% of daily calories

  • Banana with almond butter
  • Energy bar
  • Rice cakes with honey

Dinner

~20% of daily calories

Nutrition Tips

  • Time carbs around your workouts for better performance
  • Consider supplementing with protein powder and vitamins
